FAQs about Rebel Wilson

Where was Rebel Wilson born?

She was born in Sydney, Australia.

What is Rebel Wilson’s background in acting?

She trained at the Australian Theatre for Young People and at Second City in the US before beginning her career with performances on stage and television in Australia.

She made her screen debut after graduating from the Australian Theatre for Young People in 2003.

What are some of her most notable roles?

She is best known for her roles in Pitch Perfect (2012), Bridesmaids (2011), Jojo Rabbit (2019), and Cats (2019). She has also been recognized for her performance in Senior Year (2023).

Has she received any awards for her work?

Yes, she has won several awards, including the MTV Movie Award for Best Actor in a Movie and shared the Best Musical Moment award with her Pitch Perfect co-stars.

She was also nominated for the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in Jojo Rabbit among other accolades.

Did she pursue education outside of acting?

She graduated from UNSW in 2009 with a double degree in law and arts, inspired by the ’90s legal comedy-drama series, Ally McBeal.

What philanthropic efforts is she known for?

She has been involved in various charitable activities, including donating $1 million to build a theatre in Sydney and supporting The School of St Jude by sponsoring an academic scholarship and participating in fundraising events.

How did Rebel Wilson lose weight?

She embarked on a wellness regimen that included a high-protein diet, focusing on whole, unprocessed foods, lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables. She also emphasized hydration and avoiding sugar and junk food.

Has she written any books?

Yes, she has written a memoir titled Rebel Rising, which details her personal and professional journey, including her experiences with fertility issues, weight gain and loss, and her path to self-love.

Did she gamble her own money for charity?

Yes, she risked $93,000 of her own money on a question during the show Who Wants To Be A Millionaire to benefit charity. She went home with $250,000 after correctly answering the question.
