Jennifer Lawrence Is Engaged To Her Boyfriend Of Eight Months!

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Just a few months old on her relationship, Jennifer Shrader Lawrence a.k.a Jennifer Lawrence and her boyfriend took a big decision of getting engaged. The news of the newly engaged occurred when Jennifer was recently spotted flaunting her diamond ring.

American actor, Jennifer Lawrence made her name noticeable from her primary role as Lauren Pearson in the 2007 TV sitcom, The Bill Engvall Show. Besides that theTimes magazine 2013 named Jennifer as the 100 most influential people in the world.

The Silver Linings Playbook actress is officially engaged to her boyfriend, Cooke Maroney. They were dating for almost eight months from mid-2018. While the pair kept the news of their engagement a low key, the media shutterbugs were able to capture Jennifer’s diamond ring during their recent dinner date in New York City.

The photo of Jennifer and Cooke in early February 2019 (Picture Source: E Online)

The duo got first introduced by Jennifer’s friend named Laura Simpson. Since then the pair’s gradual likings towards each other started to build. Her husband-to-be works as an art dealer and chose to keep a very low appearance on the media spotlights.

The couple has not given any official announcement date of getting married. Nevertheless, Jennifer on her 2015 interview with the Vogue magazine talked that she can’t wait to get married. The actress also further added that she always wished to have a man of her dream to whom she can share children with.

Apart from Cooke, Jennifer’s boyfriend list includes her Mother director Darren Aronofsky between 2016-17. It is reported that the former split ways citing their 20 years of age gap. The Hunger Games actress also got linked with the English singer, Chris Martin in 2015. However, the rumored couple didn’t give any official statement about their possible dating. Similarly, Jennifer was also in three years of affair with actor Nicholas Hoult between 2011-2014.

Career, Net Worth & Earnings

The 28-year-old actress has mounted a colossal net worth of $130 million. She garnered the most essential part of earnings through her twelve years of acting career from 2006. Some of the movie credentials that made her grasp such humungous wealth include the 2016 Blockbuster film titled X-Men Apocalypse.

Jennifer made a good revenue as Raven in X-Men Apocalypse in 2016 (Picture Source: Vanity Fair)

Short Bio

Born on 15 August 1990, Jennifer Lawrence grew up in Indian Hills, Kentucky, United States. Her father Gary is a professional construction worker while her mother Karen (née Koch) works as a summer camp manager. Her other family member includes her two elder siblings named Ben and Blaine.
