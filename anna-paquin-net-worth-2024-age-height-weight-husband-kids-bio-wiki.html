Early Life & Biography

Paquin has two older siblings named Andrew (born 1977) and Katya (born 1980).

Paquin is of Dutch, Irish and French descent. Her family moved from Canada to New Zealand when she was only four years old. When she was young, she had several musical childhood hobbies which included; playing the Cello, Viola and piano. She also played a part in ballet, gymnastics, swimming and downhill skiing but she never had any interest in the film industry.

Paquin schooled at Raphael House Rudolf Steiner School located in Lower Hutt. In 1994-95, she was enrolled to Hutt Intermediate School then begun her high school studies at Wellington Girl’s College and graduated from Windward School in Los Angeles. She joined Columbia University but studied there for only one year and later decided to drop out to concentrate on her acting career. Her parents divorced in 1995, and she moved in with her mother.
