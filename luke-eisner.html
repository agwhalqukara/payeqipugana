Luke Eisner is a well-known American actor, social media influencer, model, singer, musician, blogger, and entrepreneur from Wisconsin. Eisner is well-known throughout the country as a member of the band VOIL. According to his social media page, Eisner conducts his music band with his friend Guss Ross. Luke is not only a musician but also an actor in the entertainment industry.

His breakout role in the Netflix original film Tall Girl established him as a household star. He began his career as a high-fashion model in the entertainment and fashion industries. He’s also worked on international advertisements for brands like Ralph Lauren, H&M, and Calvin Klein, to mention a few.

Luke Eisner- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Eisner was born in Wisconsin and reared there by his father, Bill Eisner, and mother, Denise Eisner. His father worked for Coca-Cola and was an advertising entrepreneur. His grandfather (William Eisner) was a well-known designer and entrepreneur in the advertising sector. Eisner received his education at Greendale High School. His professional career began when he began working for his cousin’s firm as a wedding DJ. Following that, he was signed by IMG Models.

Luke Eisner- Professional Career

Modeling

He was thrown into the spotlight when he began his modeling career. He was signed by IMG Models and has since appeared in a number of international advertisements. Among the well-known brands for which he has modeled are Ralph Lauren, Calvin Klein, Revolve, H&M, Stuart Weitzman, and American Eagle.

Music

Eisner is a rising talent in the music world. He is a member of the duet VOIL, which creates pop-rock music. Gus Ross, whom he met at Thornton School of Music, assisted him in the formation of the band. They debated naming themselves Goldilocks but ultimately decided against it.

VOIL just released the EP Déjà Vu (2019) and the LP Long Story Short (2020), and the group is now working on new tracks for 2021.

Since releasing their first tracks in 2018, the duo has shared the stage alongside major acts such as The Fray, Kesha, and X Ambassadors.

Acting

Luke Eisner made his acting debut in 2019 on ABC’s The Goldbergs. He had a cameo appearance in the show. In the same year, he received his first major part as Stig in the Netflix original film Tall Girl. His incredible performance earned him the unofficial title of “new teenage heartthrob” among fans.

He then appeared in a few episodes of the television show Timeline. He also appeared as a guest on the show Family Reunion.

Activism

In his own time, Luke advocates against domestic violence, and he and his family volunteer at a domestic abuse survivors’ shelter. He is quite passionate about this problem, and he believes that raising awareness about its negative implications is beneficial to society.
