What is Amy Chua's Net Worth?

Amy Chua is an American lawyer, academic, and writer who has a net worth of $5 million. She is perhaps best known for authoring her memoir Battle Hymn of the Tiger Mother.

Amy Chua was born in Champaign, Illinois in October 1962. She graduated from Harvard University where she earned her BA and JD. Chua is the John M. Duff Jr. Professor of Law at Yale Law School. She joined Yale in 2001 after she taught at Duke Law School for seven years. Amy Chua worked in corporate law at Cleary, Gottlieb, Steen & Hamilton.
