Janice Dickinson, 60, Dances on the Beach in Plunging Black Bikini

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Age is just a number! Janice Dickinson may be 60 years old, but that didn’t stop her from frolicking on the beach in Malibu, Calif., on Sunday, Aug. 16, working her teeny bikini body from every angle.

The self-proclaimed “world’s first supermodel” worked an itty-bitty Dior bikini with a plunging neckline during a serious boogie at the edge of the waterline. With a big smile on her face, Dickinson jumped up and down and waved her arms in the air for all to see.

The next day, the sassy America’s Next Top Model alum took to Instagram to share a couple of oceanside selfies—one serious, one grinning—both of which she simply captioned with the hashtags #JaniceDickinson and #Supermodel.
